Crypto arbitrage refers to the practice of taking advantage of price differences between different cryptocurrency exchanges or markets to generate profits. It involves buying a cryptocurrency on one exchange where the price is lower and selling it on another exchange where the price is higher, thereby pocketing the difference in price as profit. To date, this is the only industry that stably and legally allows you to receive from 5 to 8% profit daily or x2 of the investment amount for 2 weeks. We also often use the triangular arbitrage strategy, which involves executing a sequence of three trades on different exchanges or trading platforms in order to profit from the price difference between the three cryptocurrencies. This type of arbitrage is based on price imbalances between different cryptocurrencies.
